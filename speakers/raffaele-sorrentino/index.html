Compose Animations from A to Z

To provide the best experience to your users, animations are one of the key tools. Not only they can make your UI cleaner and more polished, but they can also make it easier for users to understand what the app is doing, hinting them to the right direction. Historically, animations have always been a pain point for Android development. However, since the introduction of Jetpack Compose in 2022, things have dramatically changed. In this talk, we will look together at the different possibilities offered by the Jetpack Compose Animation APIs.
